4 Our Approach

4.1 New Steganography Position

  • use Y cannel in YCrCb color space
    • Y cannel contan part semantic infomation
    • cr,cb contain all color infomation and part semantic information
    • use gray image as secret image to improve security
  • order
    1. covert to YCrCb
    2. encoder: Y channel and secret image connect
    3. decoder: decode Y channel of stego image
  • less affect on backpropagation: weighted computation

4.2 Basic Model

  • comparion
    • convolutional: lots of expertise and diffcult
    • deep learning: easy achieve
  • inception module: fuse feature and receptive well – tab.1
  • residual module and batch normalization: fewer training
  • a fully convolutional network as decoder: reveal the secret image
  • batch normalization: speed up – tab.2

4.3 Our steganalyzer

  • purpose: take steganalysis into account automatically
  • the statistical detectability
    • KL
    • JS
  • GAN
    • Basic GAN
    • our GAN introduce CNN-based steganalysis mode
  • spatial pyramid pooling (SPP) and variants
    • arbitrary can be input
    • extract more feature from different receptive field

4.4 Mixed Loss Function

  • MSN problem just penalizes error but disregrds human visual
  • introduce SSIM and MS-SSIM
